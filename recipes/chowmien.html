Chow mien

Description

This classic chow mein is a quick stir-fried noodle dish packed with crisp vegetables, savory sauce, and tender bites of meat or tofu. Tossed in a rich soy-based blend, it delivers bold flavor and satisfying texture in every bite—perfect for a speedy and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g chow mein noodles
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup celery (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok, sauté garlic, then add vegetables and meat/tofu.
  3. Stir-fry until veggies are tender-crisp
  4. Add noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce
  5. Toss everything well; finish with sesame oil
